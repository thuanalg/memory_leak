streamline		<-->	verb		/ˈstriːmlaɪn/
 /ˈstriːmlaɪn/
	;tinh giảm, tinh gọn -- [usually passive] streamline something to give something a smooth, even shape so that it can move quickly and easily through air or water
		;The cars all have a new streamlined design.
	;tổ chức lại -- streamline something to make a system, an organization, etc. work better, especially in a way that saves money
		;The production process is to be streamlined.
